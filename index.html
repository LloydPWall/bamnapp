What this app is

BamnApp is an informal, employee-facing shift tracker. It is not the official company clock-in or payroll system. It is a personal tool to help you track your own hours and identify any discrepancies with central records.

What data is stored

  • Your clock-in and clock-out times
  • Any manual corrections you make, including the original timestamps
  • Correction reasons you enter

Who can see your data

Nobody. Your data is stored only on this device, in your browser. No data is sent to any server. No one else can access it.

Data retention

Your data is kept for one month only. Shifts older than 30 days are automatically removed. If you need to raise a correction or discrepancy, you must do so within this window. After that, contact your manager directly.

Disputes and corrections

You can freely correct any of your own shift records. The original timestamp is always preserved. Export your data as a CSV and share it with your manager if you need to raise a discrepancy.

⚠ Your data exists only on this device and browser. If you clear your browser storage, uninstall the app, or switch devices, your shift history will be lost. Export your CSV regularly as a backup.
