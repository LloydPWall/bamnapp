What this app is

BamnApp is an informal, employee-facing shift tracker. It is not the official company clock-in or payroll system. It helps you track your own hours and identify discrepancies with central records.

What data is stored

  • Your clock-in and clock-out times
  • Any manual corrections, including original timestamps
  • Correction reasons you enter
  • Schedule data (fetched automatically when you refresh the Rota tab)
  • Reservations data (fetched from a shared source when you refresh)
  • Tip data (fetched automatically when you refresh the Tips tab)

Who can see your data

Nobody. All data is stored only on this device, in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. Schedule, bookings and tip data is fetched via a secure proxy and cached on this device only.

Data retention

Shift data is kept for 30 days only and removed automatically. Raise any corrections within this window. After that, contact your manager directly. Schedule, bookings and tip data are fetched fresh on each refresh and cached locally.

Corrections

You can freely correct any of your shift records. Original timestamps are always preserved alongside corrections. Export a CSV to share with your manager if you need to raise a discrepancy.

Tip estimates

The Tips tab shows an estimated tip share based on your logged shift times and scheduled colleague hours. These are approximations only — actual amounts may differ.

⚠ Your data lives only on this device and browser. Clearing browser storage, uninstalling, or switching devices will erase your history. Export your CSV regularly as a backup.
